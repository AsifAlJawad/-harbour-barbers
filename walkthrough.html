Book & Manage Appointments

  • View, reschedule, or cancel bookings

  • Loyalty points awarded automatically

Staff Dashboard

Daily Schedule & Walk-ins

Barbers and front-of-house staff get a real-time view of the day's appointments. They can update statuses, add walk-ins, and mark jobs complete.

  • Today's appointment list, per-barber
  • Mark as confirmed, completed, or no-show
  • Add walk-in customers instantly
  • Customer search & lookup
  • Toggle barber availability on/off
