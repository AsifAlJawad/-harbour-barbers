ABOUT US
"A great haircut isn't just about the hair. It's the 30 minutes where a man can sit down, breathe, and leave feeling like himself again."

James founded Harbour Barbers in 1992 with a simple belief: every customer deserves a barber's full attention and a cut that suits their life — not just the trend. Over three decades later, that philosophy hasn't changed.

Our team of three carries that tradition forward, each with their own style and speciality. Walk-ins are always welcome, but regulars know to book ahead.

Step through the door and you'll find the same unhurried pace, the same attention to detail, and the same honest craft that has kept locals coming back for over thirty years.

Common questions

FAQ

Do I need to book in advance?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but booking ahead guarantees your preferred barber and time slot. Saturdays fill up quickly — we recommend booking by Wednesday for the weekend.

How long does a haircut take?

A standard haircut takes around 30–40 minutes. A cut and beard combined runs closer to 50–60 minutes. We never rush — you’ll leave looking right.

Can I get a beard trim without a haircut?

Absolutely. Beard trims start from $25 and can be booked as a standalone service. Our barbers take the same care with your beard as they do with your hair.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and all major debit and credit cards. No contactless limit. Tipping is always appreciated but never expected.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Last-minute cancellations make it harder for us to accommodate walk-ins and other regulars.

Is there parking nearby?

Free street parking on Elliott Street directly outside. Easily reached from anywhere in the Lower Mainland.
